  • Then: "Now check if patient has variants in those genes"

  • Then: "Check ClinVar for clinical significance"
  • Finally: Synthesized answer

YOU didn't write that sequence. The LLM did.

So the LLM chose ONE path out of 55,986 possibilities.
Can't prove that choice was optimal (didn't check the other 55,985).
